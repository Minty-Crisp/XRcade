Minty’s XRcade

Enter a 3D arcade to play through a collection of XR mini-games with controls for VR, Desktop Keyboard|Mouse & Mobile. System and Games built with AUXL (A-Frame UX Library).

Mini Games :

  • Memory Order : Memorize and repeat an ever expanding random sequence of 4 inputs.
  • Swipe Launch : Hover on and off to build the biggest charge and click to launch before time runs out.
  • Guess Hit : Within a set amount of chances, hit all floor targets by guessing it’s approx location from 1 of 3 ceilings layouts. Progress as many levels as you can without failing.
  • Drag Diffuse : Diffuse each target by dragging from the center to each spawned bomb before they can explode. Progress through ever difficult levels by diffusing all bombs in time.
  • And more games coming soon...
